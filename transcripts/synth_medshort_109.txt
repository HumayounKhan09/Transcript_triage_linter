Agent: It covers a range of things about the condo project's overall health. Things like the percentage of units that are owner-occupied versus rented, whether there are any pending or active litigation matters involving the association, the adequacy of the association's reserves, whether the master insurance policy meets coverage requirements, the number of units in the project, and whether any single entity owns more than a certain percentage of the units.
Caller: Why does the lender care about all that?
Agent: Because the lender is making a loan on a unit within a larger project, and the financial health of the whole project affects the value and marketability of your individual unit.
